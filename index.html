THREE LAYERS | TECHNOLOGY FOR HUMANITY

To enable the vision of an Internet accessible to all and owned by everyone, the ThreeFold Network had to
review the whole Internet infrastructure - from seven to three layers, from centralized to peer-to-peer -
Humanity’s Internet is now ready.

LIFE

THE EXPERIENCE LAYER

Where users can access different sets of decentralized applications (social, office, productivity, finances, and more) and where creators can build new experiences. When human imagination come into play the possibilities are endless.

THE THREEFOLD TOKEN | A MEDIUM FOR IMPACT

Traditionally, investments were based on risk and reward. Today, the way we allocate our resources is increasingly focused on positive social and environmental impact for the highest good of humanity and our planet. 
The ThreeFold Token (TFT) contributes to a more sustainable, fair and equally distributed global Internet.

TFTs represent underlying Internet capacity and can be exchanged for that capacity anytime.  
Tokens are only generated when additional capacity is added to the ThreeFold Grid.  
No centralized issuers. No tokens created out of thin air.
