3BOT IS YOUR DIGITAL TWIN

There is nothing else quite like 3Bot. It is autonomous, neutral, and private. 3Bot lives on top of the ThreeFold Grid and manages your workloads, data, and capacity. It puts you back in control of your data and provides access to a more secure and neutral decentralized digital world.

FEATURES

In order to truly act on your behalf, 3Bot was designed with a suite of capabilities that will assist you towards the daily digital needs of you and/or your organization. Your 3Bot is peer2peer – it efficiently and securely connects directly to other 3Bots, without any intermediaries between you and your self-designated circles. It manages, protects, and exchanges data, value, and information – only when authorized by you.

Your 3Bot is stateless – there is no longer any need to store a single piece of information several times, as 3Bot  autonomously stores your data across the Grid in an intelligent and efficient manner. You get back ownership of your data, without having to manage its distribution.

DIGITAL IDENTITY

One digital identity, not many spread across multiple sites. Your information is stored and managed by yourself, not in a centralized database. You decide who can access your information by defining your own circles. You are in control.

VIRTUAL (SYS)ADMIN

3Bot is a digital assistant that fulfills key online duties for you. Whether it be reserving capacity, getting in touch with your designated circles, or managing your information and data, 3Bot carries out key administrative functions.

EVOLUTIONARY

An extensive, integrated  SDK means that 3Bot can be taught by developers and system integrators to become a versatile digital assistant for your personal life and any other digital services.
