SOME OF OUR CORE VALUES

SUSTAINABILITY

Sustainability ensures the future of life on earth. It is about adopting the behavior and mindset to minimize our footprint.

We need an Internet that is environmentally conscious, where networks, hardware, and software components, as well as processes are optimized around energy-efficiency and carbon neutrality as much as possible.

FREEDOM

Freedom of thought, opinion, creativity, and faith are fundamentals to achieve peace and fulfilment of humankindâ€™s potential.

The Internet should be responsible and respectful of individual privacy, and provide freedom to all stakeholders. We enable everyone to own and manage their data just like they own their experiences in reality. 

EQUALITY

Equality is the foundation for a fair world where everyone is given the opportunity to be empowered and to achieve their full potential. The Internet should be inclusive to all of humanity.

We are building an Internet that is owned by many, can be deployed everywhere, and is accessible to all. Everyone should enjoy a fair return for their provided value.

A STRONG HISTORY AND A BOLD VISION

We have expertise in storage, automation, and cloud. From 2000 to 2015 and through seven exits totalling $500M, before ThreeFold came to be, key stakeholders defined parts of the cloud landscape. Some of those elements are still being actively used today.

We believe the world needs a decentralized Internet, to bring sustainable and neutral access to information and education for our generations and those to come. And we at ThreeFold are committed to providing you with the best complementary solutions.

Where We Come From

2000s

Before ThreeFold saw first daylight, predecessor companies were making waves in the storage, automation, and cloud computing landscapes.

2016

Three separate elements (Zero OS, Rivine, and JumpScale) were created and with time turned into the three core aspects of the TF Grid.

2017

The concept of the ThreeFold Network becomes a reality, through the publication of white paper 0.9. Release of the TF Chain. First tokens are created.

2018

The first batches of capacity are added to the TF Grid in different parts of Europe and the UAE, soon followed by the U.S. and Canada.

2018

ThreeFold signs two major partnerships (HPE & Solidaridad) in short succession and signs on a major capacity farmer.

2019

The ThreeFold Grid goes into beta and the first test users are taking it for a spin.

2020

The TF Grid goes public. The ecosystem expands with the help of partners and farmers. Developers build on top of the TF Grid, growing the Autonomous Internet.

2025

1 Billion people around the world have access to a green, fast, and affordable Internet and education solutions.

2030

The vision of a fully decentralized and democratized Internet, for you and owned by all of us, is achieved.

AN OPEN ECOSYSTEM

Hewlett Packard Enterprise (HPE) provides their clients in the CEEMA region the option to connect their computing and storage capacity to ThreeFold's decentralized grid.

Most HPE server and storage hardware have been certified to be used on the ThreeFold Grid and HPE plans to build services to support the growth and usage of the ThreeFold Grid.

Visit HPE

Kleos is a 5G telecommunication technology helping ThreeFold scale to serve the last mile in connectivity.

Kleos is particularly critical in low income countries where Internet connectivity is expensive and inaccessible.

ThreeFold technology is approved to be used within the base stations they deploy.

Visit Kleos

Solidaridad accelerates solutions for more sustainable and more inclusive supply chains.

They work closely with farmers and partner with companies, financial institutions, governments, service providers, and knowledge institutions around them.

ThreeFold capacity will be deployed in a pilot program for cocoa farmers in Ghana, in locations owned and operated by Solidaridad and local service providers.

Visit Solidaridad

FlowGen is a renewable energy company that applied its extensive aerodynamic know-how from the automotive racing industry into the wind industry to optimize the design of a 5-meter wind turbine. These turbines can be installed virtually anywhere without the need for high masts or deep foundations. This makes it possible to cost-effectively deliver power nearly anywhere in the world.

Together with FlowGen, ThreeFold can push Internet capacity for a fair price to the places that need it the most.

Visit FlowGen

Take Action Global (TAG) has created a global network of educators helping to create several innovation lab schools around the world, and has launched impactful climate action projects.

TAG has been providing IT capabilities (laptops and software) to schools, cities, and countries, and finding teachers around the world who would commit their time and effort in teaching.

ThreeFold will offer financial commitment to TAG. Also Threefold will offer the support of technology. This involves giving access to Adapps, creating capacity on the network and moving towards a neutral and decentralized work environment.

Visit TAG
