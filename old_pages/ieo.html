ABOUT THE IEO

What is an initial exchange offering (IEO)

An Initial Exchange Offering (IEO) is a token sale supervised by a cryptocurrency exchange. IEOs are available exclusively to the exchange's users. IEOs allow investors to get new cryptocurrencies (or tokens) while raising funds for promising projects. February 2020 we are launching an IEO for the ThreeFold Token on Liquid.com.
